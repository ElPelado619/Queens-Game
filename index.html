Queens Game

Rules

  • Place one queen in every region of color to win.

  • Click on a square to place a queen or remove one.

  • Right click to place a marker or remove one.

  • You can drag the mouse to draw on the board.

  • Make sure no queens are in the same row or column.

  • Queens can be on the same diagonal.

  • Queens can't be adyacent to each other.
